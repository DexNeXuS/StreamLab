Touch Portal

Touch Portal: layouts, pages, and integrations

Touch Portal gives you buttons, pages, and integrations for stream ops, scene control, and quick automation. Connect it to Streamer.bot to trigger actions from your phone or tablet.

Official: Touch Portal — download and docs.

How to set up

Install Touch Portal on your PC and (optionally) the mobile app. Add a server in Touch Portal and connect Streamer.bot so you can trigger actions from buttons. Optional image: assets/images/page-images/touch-portal-setup.png.

Examples

Button layouts for scene switching, command toggles, or Get Commands. Add assets/images/page-images/touch-portal-example.png to show your layout.
