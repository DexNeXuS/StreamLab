StreamElements

Overlays, loyalty, alerts, and the bits that plug into Twitch. Use it alongside your own HTML widgets and Streamer.bot so overlays and loyalty stay in sync with the rest of your setup.

How to set up

Connect StreamElements to your Twitch account, add overlays and alerts in the dashboard, and add the overlay URLs as browser sources in OBS. Optional image: assets/images/page-images/streamelements-setup.png.
